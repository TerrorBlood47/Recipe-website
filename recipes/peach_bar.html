Homemade Peach Crumb Bar

Peach crumb bars, specifically, became popular as a delicious way to showcase the natural sweetness and flavor of ripe peaches. They are often enjoyed during the summer months when peaches are in season and at their best.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 cups fresh peaches, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar (for peach filling)
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, 1 cup of granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. n a small bowl, beat the egg and vanilla extract together. Add the egg mixture to the flour mixture and mix until well combined. The dough will be crumbly.
  4. Press half of the dough into the bottom of the greased baking dish, making an even layer.
  5. In another bowl, mix the sliced peaches, 1/2 cup of granulated sugar, cornstarch, and ground cinnamon until well coated.
  6. Spread the peach mixture evenly over the crust in the baking dish.
  7. Sprinkle the remaining dough mixture evenly over the peaches.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 45 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the peach filling is bubbling.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely before cutting into squares.
  10. Serve the peach crumb bars at room temperature or chilled. You can also dust them with powdered sugar before serving, if desired.
