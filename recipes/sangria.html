Sangria! Sangria!

Sangria is a popular Spanish drink that combines red wine, fresh fruits, and other flavorful ingredients. It's perfect for gatherings or as a refreshing summer beverage.

Feel free to adjust the sweetness or add different fruits according to your taste preferences. Cheers!

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle of red wine (such as a medium-bodied red like Rioja or Merlot)
  • 1/4 cup brandy or orange liqueur (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 orange, sliced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 lime, sliced
  • 1 apple, cored and sliced
  • 1 cup sparkling water or lemon-lime soda (optional, for added fizz)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher, combine the red wine, brandy, orange liqueur, and sugar. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add the sliced orange, lemon, apple, and mixed fresh fruits to the pitcher. Gently stir to combine all the ingredients.
  3. Cover the pitcher and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. When ready to serve, fill glasses with ice cubes. Pour the chilled sangria into the glasses, including some of the fruit slices.
  5. If desired, top each glass with a splash of soda water or lemon-lime soda to add a fizzy touch.
  6. Stir gently and garnish with additional fruit slices, if desired.
  7. Enjoy your refreshing homemade Sangria!
